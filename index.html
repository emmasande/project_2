A highly versatile and modern sans-serif typeface designed by Mark Simonson. It exudes a clean and contemporary aesthetic, making it widely favored for various design applications, from print to digital media. Its geometric proportions and balanced letterforms contribute to excellent legibility in both small and large sizes, while its extensive range of weights and styles offers designers ample flexibility for creative expression. With its subtle yet distinctive characteristics, Proxima Nova is often chosen for branding, editorial layouts, web design, and user interfaces, embodying a perfect blend of functionality and elegance.
